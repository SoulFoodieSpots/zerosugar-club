Is the no-sugar diet safe and effective?

Yes! Research shows that reducing added sugars (not natural sugars from fruits) improves weight loss, blood sugar control, and cardiovascular health. Our program follows American Heart Association guidelines: under 25g daily for women, 36g for men.

Can I eat fruit on this program?

Absolutely! Our meal plans include whole fruits, which contain natural sugars plus fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. We eliminate added sugars from processed foods, sodas, and sweets‚Äînot the healthy sugars found in whole foods.

Unlike restrictive keto (which eliminates healthy whole grains and fruits), our approach is based on Mediterranean-style eating with low added sugar. Stanford research shows this method achieves similar weight loss with better long-term adherence and heart health benefits.
